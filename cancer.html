Cancer, symbolized by the crab, is known for their nurturing and protective nature. Like the different colors of bell peppers, Cancerians exhibit a range of emotions and qualities, from tender sweetness to fiery passion. They can be gentle and compassionate like the green bell pepper, offering support and comfort to those around them. At the same time, they possess a fiery intensity akin to the red bell pepper when defending their loved ones or pursuing their goals.

Much like the thick flesh of bell peppers provides protection for the seeds within, Cancerians prioritize creating a safe and secure environment for themselves and their loved ones. Their protective shell, like that of the bell pepper, shields them from external harm while allowing their inner warmth and kindness to shine through. Cancerians play a vital role in their social circles, often bringing harmony and nourishment to those they care about.
